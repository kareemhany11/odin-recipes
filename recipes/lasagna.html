lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

steps:

  1. rep: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Boil noodles per package instructions.
  2. Cook beef: Brown beef with garlic, drain fat, then mix in marinara sauce.
  3. Cheese mix: Combine ricotta, egg, basil, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  4. Layer: In a 9x13" dish: Sauce → Noodles → Ricotta mix → Mozzarella. Repeat 3x.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil, bake 25 mins. Uncover, bake 10 more mins.
  6. Rest 10 mins before serving.
